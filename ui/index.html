Privacy

BrainSprint is designed with your privacy as a core principle. All data — including your writings, session history, and app preferences — is stored locally on your device and never leaves your computer. There are no accounts, no telemetry, no analytics, and no cloud sync. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party. Your words are yours alone.
